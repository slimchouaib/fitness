For children (ages 6-12)

Warm-up:
5 minutes of jogging or jumping jacks.
Circuit 1:
30 seconds of squats, 30 seconds of push-ups, 30 seconds of jumping jacks, 30 seconds of bicycle crunches.
Circuit 2:
30 seconds of lunges, 30 seconds of burpees, 30 seconds of high knees, 30 seconds of hula-hooping.
Cool-down:
5 minutes of stretching.

For teenagers (ages 13-19)

Warm-up:
5 minutes of jogging or jump rope.
Circuit 1:
30 seconds of lunges, 30 seconds of burpees, 30 seconds of mountain climbers, 30 seconds of plank holds.
Circuit 2:
30 seconds of jump squats, 30 seconds of push-ups, 30 seconds of high knees, 30 seconds of bicycle crunches.
Cool-down:
5 minutes of stretching.

View Schedule

For adults (ages 20-59)

Warm-up:
5-10 minutes of cardio (running, cycling, jumping jacks, etc.)
Resistance training:
3 sets of 12 reps for each of the following exercises: squats, lunges, bench press, rows, overhead press, bicep curls, tricep extensions, and planks.
Cardio intervals:
30 seconds of high-intensity exercise (such as sprinting or jumping jacks) followed by 30 seconds of rest for 10-15 minutes.
Cool-down:
5-10 minutes of stretching.

View Schedule

For seniors (ages 60 and above)

Warm-up:
5-10 minutes of walking or gentle cycling.
Resistance training:
3 sets of 12 reps for each of the following exercises using resistance bands: bicep curls, tricep extensions, squats, lunges, rows, and chest presses.
Balance exercises:
standing on one leg for 30 seconds, heel-to-toe walking for 10 steps, and seated leg extensions for 12 reps.
Cool-down:
5-10 minutes of gentle stretching and breathing exercises.
